About:

Welcome to Particles and Waves lab. We are a group of fluid dynamicists interested in answering fundamental questions about the nature of flows. We use a combination of analytical and computational tools to study these phenomena. The two broad categories we work on are

  • Particle laden flows
  • Geophysical flows
Department of Applied Mechanics and Biomedical Engineering
